### Chromebook Reality (School Programs)

**Government "One Tablet Per Child" Evolution**:
- 2012-2016: Tablet distribution program
- 2020+: Shifted to Chromebook adoption
- Current fleet: Mix of 2-4GB RAM devices

**Common Chromebook Specs**:
```
Low-end (60% of fleet):
├── RAM: 2GB
├── Storage: 16-32GB
├── CPU: ARM Cortex-A / Intel Celeron
├── WebGPU: Not supported

Mid-range (30% of fleet):
├── RAM: 4GB
├── Storage: 32-64GB
├── CPU: Intel N-series / AMD
├── WebGPU: Partial support

├── RAM: 8GB
```

## 6. Cultural Considerations

### Thai Educational Culture

**Respect hierarchy (เกรงใจ)**:
- Students hesitant to ask questions
- Fear of appearing ignorant
- Socratic method addresses this (AI has no judgment)

**Face-saving (หน้า)**:
- Private tutoring preferred over public struggle
- AI tutoring = no social embarrassment
- Individual progress tracking important

**Collective learning**:
- Study groups common
- Future feature: collaborative modes

### Thai Language Requirements

**Critical for adoption**:
- Full Thai UI required
- Thai explanations for STEM concepts
- Thai mathematical terminology
- Cultural examples (Thai context problems)

**Tokenization challenges**:
- Thai script: no spaces between words
- PyThaiNLP for word segmentation
- ~1.5-2x token overhead vs English

## 9. Recommendations

### Must-Have Features

1. **Thai-first experience**: Full Thai UI and AI responses
2. **Offline capability**: Work without constant connection
3. **Mobile optimization**: Smartphone is primary device
4. **Low storage footprint**: <500MB initial download
5. **Progressive download**: Don't block usage during download

### Architecture Alignment

Based on market research, the **hybrid tiered architecture** is validated:

```
Tier Distribution (estimated):
├── Cloud-only (low-end phones, 2GB Chromebooks): ~40%
├── Hybrid (4GB Chromebooks, mid-range phones): ~35%
└── Full edge (8GB+ devices): ~25%
```
