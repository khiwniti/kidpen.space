<objective>
Build a powerful dashboard for teachers to manage their classes and students. This includes class-wide mastery heatmaps, individual student progress tracking, and identifying students needing intervention.
</objective>

## Task 1: Class-Wide Mastery Heatmap
- **read_first**: `apps/frontend/src/components/dashboard/`
- **action**: Create a `ClassHeatmap` component showing students vs concepts.
- **acceptance_criteria**:
  - Highlights concepts that the class as a whole is struggling with.
  - Allows drilling down into a specific student or concept.

## Task 2: Student Progress Comparison
- **read_first**: `packages/shared/src/mastery/types.ts`
- **action**: Implement a "Class Overview" showing a distribution of mastery levels (Novice to Mastered).
- **acceptance_criteria**:
  - Helps teachers identify students falling behind or excelling.
  - Groups students by current topic.

## Task 3: Student Intervention Alerts
- **read_first**: `packages/shared/src/mastery/difficulty.ts`
- **action**: Create an "Intervention" panel that lists students with stagnant pyBKT scores.
- **acceptance_criteria**:
  - Notifies teachers of students who might need 1-on-1 support.
  - Suggests specific concepts to review with each student.

---

## Verification Steps

1. Log in as a teacher and view a class's heatmap.
2. Verify students are correctly mapped to their mastery scores.
3. Check "Intervention Alerts" for students with low progress.
