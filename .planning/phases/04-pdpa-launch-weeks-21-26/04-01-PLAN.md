<objective>
Ensure kidpen.space is fully compliant with Thailand's Data Protection Act (PDPA). This involves explicit consent management, clear privacy policies, and the implementation of user rights like data deletion and portability.
</objective>

## Task 1: Consent Management Flow
- **read_first**: `apps/frontend/src/app/auth/`
- **action**: Add a "Consent" step to the registration/first-login flow.
- **acceptance_criteria**:
  - Explicit checkbox for "Terms of Service" and "Privacy Policy".
  - Consent is recorded in Supabase (metadata or separate table).

## Task 2: Account Deletion & Right to be Forgotten
- **read_first**: `apps/frontend/src/app/(dashboard)/settings/`
- **action**: Implement a "Delete Account" button that triggers data erasure from Supabase and Google Drive (if connected).
- **acceptance_criteria**:
  - User's profile, mastery data, and session history are wiped.
  - Confirmation dialog with clear warnings.

## Task 3: Data Portability (Export)
- **read_first**: `apps/frontend/src/lib/drive/api-client.ts`
- **action**: Add a "Download My Data" button that generates a ZIP of all learning state.
- **acceptance_criteria**:
  - Mastery data, session history, and preferences are included in JSON format.

## Task 4: Localized Privacy Policy & TOS
- **read_first**: Thai PDPA standards
- **action**: Create `apps/frontend/src/app/(home)/privacy/page.tsx` and `apps/frontend/src/app/(home)/terms/page.tsx` with Thai content.
- **acceptance_criteria**:
  - Legal documents are clear, accessible, and in Thai.
